About Us

eta hacks is a hackathon created to inspire and motivate coders to exercise and develop their programming skills. Over a span of 30 hours, teams of up to 4 participants will work to develop a software project that aims to solve a real world problem.
despite being virtual, we still guarantee an amazing experience, with the same level of fun, experience, competition, and opporunities as an in-person hackathon!
